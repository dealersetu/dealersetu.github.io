# Assigning Vehicle

This guide explains how an **EDP (Electronic Data Processor)** can assign a vehicle to a sales manager using an **OTF number** (which is linked to the customer) in the **DealerSetu** system.

## Overview

Use this process to **assign a vehicle** in **DealerSetu**.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Click on Free Stock

![Assigning vehicle Step 1](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step1.png)

Click on **Free Stock** to find a free, unassigned vehicle.  
In case you need to assign an **In-Stock** vehicle, you can do that as well.

---

### Step 2: Find the Desired Vehicle

![Assigning vehicle Step 2](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step2.png)

The **Booking Coordinator** will provide the **Model Code**.  
Look for the vehicle with the given model code and check its availability.

---

### Step 3: Click on Edit

![Assigning vehicle Step 3](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step3.png)

Click on the **Edit** option for the selected vehicle.

---

### Step 4: Add OTF Number

![Assigning vehicle Step 4](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step4.png)

Enter the **OTF Number** provided by the **Booking Coordinator**.  
If you are assigning an already assigned vehicle, you can **replace the existing OTF number**.

---

### Step 5: Click on Save

![Assigning vehicle Step 5](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step4.png)

Click **Save** to complete the process.  
This will assign the **vehicle to the customer** linked with the provided **OTF number**.
