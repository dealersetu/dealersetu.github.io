# Transit Vehicle

This guide explains how an **EDP (Electronic Data Processor)** can **mark a vehicle as Transit** to another **Dealership** in the **DealerSetu** system.

## Overview

Use this process to **mark a vehicle as in transit** in **DealerSetu**.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Click on the Edit Button

![Transit vehicle Step 1](/img/dealersetu/edp/transit/step1.png)

Navigate to the vehicle you want to mark as **In Transit** and click the **Edit** icon.

---

### Step 2: Select the In Transit Checkbox

![Transit vehicle Step 2](/img/dealersetu/edp/transit/step2.png)

Enable the **In Transit** checkbox for the selected vehicle.

---

### Step 3: Click on Save

![Transit vehicle Step 3](/img/dealersetu/edp/transit/step3.png)

The vehicle will now appear under the **Transit** bucket.
