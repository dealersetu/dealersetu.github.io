# Creating a New Stock Yard

This guide explains how an **EDP (Electronic Data Processor)** can create a **new Stock Yard** in the **DealerSetu** system.

## Overview

Use this process to create and manage a **new stock yard** in DealerSetu.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Select Masters

![Creating New Stock Yard Step 1](/img/dealersetu/edp/creating_new_stock_yard/step1.png)

Navigate to the **Master** section.

---

### Step 2: Select Y Stock Yards

![Creating New Stock Yard Step 2](/img/dealersetu/edp/creating_new_stock_yard/step2.png)

Open the **Y Stock Yards** section.

---

### Step 3: Click on Create New

![Creating New Stock Yard Step 3](/img/dealersetu/edp/creating_new_stock_yard/step3.png)

Click **Create New** to add a stock yard.

---

### Step 4: Fill in the Details

![Creating New Stock Yard Step 4](/img/dealersetu/edp/creating_new_stock_yard/step4.png)

Enter all required **stock yard details**.

---

### Step 5: Click on Save

![Creating New Stock Yard Step 5](/img/dealersetu/edp/creating_new_stock_yard/step5.png)

Click **Save** to create the **new Stock Yard**.
