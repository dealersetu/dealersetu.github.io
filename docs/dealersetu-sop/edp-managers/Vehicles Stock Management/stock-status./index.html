Stock Statuses

This guide explains how an EDP (Electronic Data Processor) can view vehicle stock filtered by different statuses in the DealerSetu system.

Overview​

The New Vehicle page contains six stock status buckets that help you track vehicles based on their current state.

Sold​

Displays all vehicles that have been sold.

In Stock​

Shows vehicles that are assigned to a Sales Manager.

Free Stock​

Displays all unassigned vehicles that are available for assignment.

Processing​

Shows vehicles that are currently under processing.

Transit​

Displays vehicles that are on their way to the dealership.

Transferred​

Shows vehicles that are being transferred to another dealership.
