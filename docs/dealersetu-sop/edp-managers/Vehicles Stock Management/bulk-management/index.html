Bulk Managing Vehicle in Stock

This guide explains how an EDP (Electronic Data Processor) can import and export vehicles in stock in the DealerSetu system.

Overview​

Use this process to bulk manage vehicle details in the stock inventory in DealerSetu.

Step-by-Step Process​

Step 1: Click on Stock​

Navigate to the Stock section.

Step 2: Select New Vehicle​

Choose the New Vehicle option.

Step 3: Click on the Import Button​

Step 4: Download the Template​

Download the template file for bulk vehicle upload.

Step 5: Prepare and Upload the File​

Update the vehicle data according to the template and upload the file.

Step 6: Click on Import New Vehicles​

Start the import process for the uploaded file.

Step 7: Verify the Import​

Verify the number of successfully imported and skipped records.

Exporting Stock Data​

Click on Export​

This will download all stock data to your system.
