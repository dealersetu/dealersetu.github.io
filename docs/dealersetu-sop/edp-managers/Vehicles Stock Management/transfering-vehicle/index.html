Transfer Vehicles

This guide explains how an EDP (Electronic Data Processor) can transfer a vehicle to another Dealership in the DealerSetu system.

Overview​

Use this process to transfer a vehicle from one dealership to another in DealerSetu.

Step-by-Step Process​

Step 1: Click on Stock​

Navigate to the Stock section.

Step 2: Select New Vehicle​

Choose the New Vehicle option.

Step 3: Click on the Transfer Button​

Click the Transfer button for the selected vehicle.

Step 4: Update Details and Click Save​

Update the transfer details and click Save.

Step 5: Print Invoice​

Print the transfer invoice after saving the details.

Step 6: Select the Transfers Tab​

Navigate to the Transfers tab to view transferred vehicles.

Step 7: Click on GST Invoice​

Open the GST Invoice for the transferred vehicle.

Step 8: Print GST Invoice​

Print the GST Invoice for record-keeping.
