# Managing Vehicle in Stock

This guide explains how an **EDP (Electronic Data Processor)** can manage an existing vehicle stock entry in the DealerSetu system.

## Overview

Use this process to update vehicle details in the stock inventory in DealerSetu.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Click on Stock

![Managing vehicle Step 1](/img/dealersetu/edp/managing_vehicle/step1.png)

Navigate to the **Stock** section.

---

### Step 2: Select New Vehicle

![Managing vehicle Step 2](/img/dealersetu/edp/managing_vehicle/step2.png)

Choose the **New Vehicle** option.

---

### Step 3: Click on the Edit Icon

![Managing vehicle Step 3](/img/dealersetu/edp/managing_vehicle/step3.png)

Select the vehicle you want to update and click the **Edit** icon.

---

### Step 4: Add or Update the Details

![Managing vehicle Step 4](/img/dealersetu/edp/managing_vehicle/step4.png)

Update the required vehicle details.

---

### Step 5: Click on Save

![Managing vehicle Step 5](/img/dealersetu/edp/managing_vehicle/step5.png)

Click **Save** to apply the changes.
