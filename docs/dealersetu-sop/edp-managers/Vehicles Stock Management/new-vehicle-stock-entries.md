# Adding a New Vehicle to Stock

This guide explains how an **EDP (Electronic Data Processor)** can create a new vehicle stock entry in the DealerSetu system.

## Overview

Use this process to add a new vehicle to the stock inventory in DealerSetu.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Click on Stock

![Adding new vehicle Step 1](/img/dealersetu/edp/new_vehicle_stock_entry/step1.png)

Navigate to the **Stock** section.

---

### Step 2: Select New Vehicle

![Adding new vehicle Step 2](/img/dealersetu/edp/new_vehicle_stock_entry/step2.png)

Choose the option a new vehicle.

---

### Step 3: Click on Create New

![Adding new vehicle Step 3](/img/dealersetu/edp/new_vehicle_stock_entry/step3.png)

Click **Create New** to begin entering vehicle details.

---

### Step 4: Add All the Details

![Adding new vehicle Step 4](/img/dealersetu/edp/new_vehicle_stock_entry/step4.png)

Fill in all required vehicle information.

---

### Step 5: Click on Save

![Adding new vehicle Step 5](/img/dealersetu/edp/new_vehicle_stock_entry/step5.png)

Click **Save** to add the vehicle to stock.
