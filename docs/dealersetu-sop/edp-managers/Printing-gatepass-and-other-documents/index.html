Assigning Vehicle

This guide explains how an EDP (Electronic Data Processor) can print pass DealerSetu system.

Overview​

Use this process to Print documents in DealerSetu.

Step-by-Step Process​

Step 1: Go to cost sheet Menu cick on cost sheet​

Navigate to the Stock section.

Step 2: Click on gate pass​

This will automatically generate a gate pass and you can print it directly.

Step 3: Click on the SLD RTO​

Step 4: Fill details and hit generate​

This will automatically generate a SLD RTO and you can print it directly.

Step 5: CLick on E.S RTO​

It will create a printable E.S RTO form.

Step 6: Click on print​

Navigate to the Transfers tab to view transferred vehicles.

Step 7: Click on GST Invoice​

Open the GST Invoice .

Step 8: Print GST Invoice​

Print the GST Invoice for record-keeping.
