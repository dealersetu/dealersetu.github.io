# Updating Costsheet

This guide explains how an **EDP (Electronic Data Processor)** can update a **cost sheet** in the **DealerSetu** system.

## Overview

Use this process to **update the cost sheet** in **DealerSetu**.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Click on Cost Sheet Menu

![Updating Costsheet Step 1](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step1.png)

Navigate to the **Cost Sheet** menu from the dashboard.

---

### Step 2: Click on View Cost Sheet

![Updating Costsheet Step 2](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step2.png)

This will display the **list of all available cost sheets**.

---

### Step 3: Click on Edit

![Updating Costsheet Step 3](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step3.png)

Click on the **Edit** option for the vehicle whose cost sheet you want to update.

---

### Step 4: Update the Costsheet

![Updating Costsheet Step 4](/img/dealersetu/edp/assigning-vehicle/step4.png)

Make the required changes in the **cost sheet details**.  
Ensure all values are accurate before saving.

> **Note:** Any critical changes may require **CEO approval**, depending on system rules.

---

### Step 5: Click on Save

![Updating Costsheet Step 5](/img/dealersetu/edp/costsheet-updation/step4.png)

Click **Save** to complete the process.  
The **cost sheet will be updated successfully**.
